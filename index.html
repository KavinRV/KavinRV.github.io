About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, advised by Dr. Pawan Goyal. I am interested in developing viable alternatives that enable models to operate at the level of concepts — in a way that is less dependent on surface form and more naturally extensible across languages and modalities such as speech and images. Toward this goal, I study how concepts emerge, evolve, and are encoded within token-level representations, with a particular interest in byte-level models and concept-level architectures that process information at a more fundamental semantic level. More broadly, my work extends to diffusion language models, composed image retrieval, generative retrieval, and LLM-based embeddings.
